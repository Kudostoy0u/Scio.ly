# Anatomy – Urinary/Excretory (2026)

## Overview
Kidney anatomy, nephron function, fluid/electrolyte balance, and acid–base.

## High-Yield Topics
- Nephron segments; transporters; countercurrent mechanisms
- GFR and filtration fraction; RAAS; ADH and aquaporins
- Acid–base handling (HCO₃⁻ reabsorption; NH₄⁺)
- Disorders: AKI prerenal/intrinsic/postrenal basics

## Study Roadmap
1) Trace filtrate and major transport processes per segment
2) Hormonal regulation scenarios (RAAS/ADH)
3) Acid–base prediction questions
