# Legal Directory

This directory contains legal pages for the Scio.ly platform, including privacy policy and terms of service.

## Files

### `privacy/page.tsx`
Privacy policy page component.

**Key Features:**
- Privacy policy content
- Data collection information
- User rights and protections
- GDPR compliance information

**Important Notes:**
- Static legal content page
- Displays privacy policy text
- Theme-aware styling

### `terms/page.tsx`
Terms of service page component.

**Key Features:**
- Terms of service content
- User agreement information
- Platform usage terms
- Legal disclaimers

**Important Notes:**
- Static legal content page
- Displays terms of service text
- Theme-aware styling

## Important Notes

1. **Legal Compliance**: Pages contain required legal documentation
2. **Static Content**: Legal pages with static content
3. **Theme Support**: Dark/light mode support
4. **Accessibility**: WCAG compliant legal pages
