# Div. C Chemistry Lab Equipment List C

See General Rules, Eye Protection & other Policies on www.soinc.org as they apply to every event.

This list outlines common equipment that may be encountered or required in Division C Chemistry Lab events. Familiarity with the proper use, safety precautions, and limitations of this equipment is essential.

## General Lab Equipment
- Beakers (various sizes)
- Erlenmeyer flasks (various sizes)
- Graduated cylinders (various sizes)
- Test tubes and test tube rack
- Watch glass
- Funnel
- Stirring rod
- Spatula/Scoopula
- Wash bottle
- Pipettes (Mohr, volumetric, serological) and bulbs
- Burettes and burette clamps
- Ring stand with iron rings and clamps
- Hot plate/Stirrer
- Bunsen burner (with striker and hose)
- Wire gauze with ceramic center
- Tripod stand
- Crucible and lid
- Clay triangle
- Tongs (crucible, beaker)
- Forceps
- Evaporating dish
- Mortar and pestle
- Centrifuge

## Measurement Tools
- Balances (top-loading, analytical)
- Thermometers
- pH meter or pH paper
- Conductivity meter
- Spectrophotometer/Colorimeter (basic operation)
- Barometer

## Safety Equipment
- Safety goggles/glasses
- Lab coats/aprons
- Gloves (nitrile, latex)
- Fume hood (understanding its function)
- Fire extinguisher (location and basic use)
- Safety shower and eyewash station (location and basic use)

## Common Glassware & Plasticware
- Volumetric flasks
- Separatory funnel
- Condenser (e.g., Liebig, West)
- Distillation apparatus components
- Reagent bottles
- Dropping bottles
- Syringes

## Other Relevant Items
- Filter paper and filter funnels
- Drying oven
- Desiccator
- Stopwatch/Timer
- Heating mantle
- Vacuum filtration apparatus

Competitors should be prepared to identify, select, and safely use this equipment in various experimental setups. Knowledge of calibration procedures and common sources of error associated with each piece of equipment is also expected.
